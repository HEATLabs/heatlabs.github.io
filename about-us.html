About PCWStats

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind the project

Our Mission

PCWStats was created to provide Project CW players with comprehensive, accurate, and easy-to-understand statistics and gameplay information. We aim to be the go-to resource for both new and experienced players looking to improve their gameplay.

Our platform aggregates data from various sources and presents it in a user-friendly format, helping players make informed decisions about tank selection, tactics, and team strategies.

The Project

PCWStats started as a passion project by SINEWAVE and has grown into a community-driven initiative with contributions from players across the Project CW community.

As a beta project, we're constantly working to improve and expand our database with the most up-to-date information. We welcome feedback and suggestions from the community to help shape the future of PCWStats.

Key Features

Comprehensive Database

Explore full tank stats, including armor layout, firepower, mobility, and other key performance data.

Map Knowledge

Get tactical insight for every map, learn choke points, ambush zones, and positioning strategies.

Community Driven

Every guide, update, and feature is shaped by input from players across the Project CW community.

Player Statistics

Track your performance over time with detailed stats, progress graphs, and history data.

Always Free

No ads, no premium tiers, this project is and always will be 100% free for everyone to enjoy fully.

Fully Open Source

The code is public and open to all. Fork it, improve it, or contribute to help shape the future together.

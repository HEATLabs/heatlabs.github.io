Tournament Recap

After analysis, 3Mid was this tournament's powerhouse only losing 0-2 to Large in the first final match. Large, the team with the most competitive experience, was on the easy track to the finals until their run-in with Pincer. With Pincer's narrow win, Large had to crawl back through the losers bracket. TEAM48 was another force to be reckoned with as they had an easy path to semifinals, eventually meeting their match with 3Mid. Then losing to Large in round 8 of the losers bracket, placing third.

Overall this tournament had a lot of interesting matches and teams with potential. Experiencing upsets and comebacks 3Mid, Large, and TEAM48 deserve to be on this tournament's podium. Congratulations to all the teams giving a valiant effort.

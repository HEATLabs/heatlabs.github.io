Introduction

Last, but certainly not least, we have our final two (announced) Agents for the upcoming playtest. Heads up @News fans, coming to you today is Reaper and Udarnik. In our last playtest you would have seen them named as 'Ares' and 'Udarnik Defender' respectively.

Both Reaper and Udarnik will need to be unlocked using credits earned in-game - the amount needed is reduced for the playtest to make them more quickly attainable. We're looking forward to hearing your thoughts on these Agents and the changes they've gone through! You can find a brief introduction to these two Agents below.

Udarnik

New Agents #2

Voreksis

Former Community Manager

Official Discord Server

Introduction

It's time again for another News drop! Today we have the next two Agents that will be available for you to play in the upcoming playtest. You may recognize them from the last playtest as the Jäger Support and Jäger Marksmen - now known as Blitz and Jäger.

Jäger will be available day one with no unlocking necessary. Blitz however will need to be unlocked using credits earned in-game - this value is reduced for the playtest to make it more quickly attainable. We're looking forward to hearing your feedback on these Agents and their updates. Check out below for some insight into each of them.

Blitz

A nerd on a tank: he's a brilliant engineer ready to prove to his US counterparts that West Germany engineering is the best. The problem: he's too young to be taken seriously, especially by his (much) older brother-in-arms Jäger. And there are sometimes "extra" parts that pop up right after he reassembles that cutting-edge device…

Jäger

A hunter who never gets lost, in a German forest, or in a Middle Eastern desert. And he never misses his prey, while he knows how to make the opponents miss his vehicle. Just don't start talking to him about the fancy new gadgets — there's nothing like a good ol' trained eye for him. No one can convince him that future wars will be fought by robots.

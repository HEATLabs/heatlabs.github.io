PATCH NOTES #8

Kent • XM1 90

Firepower

  • Fixed camera zoom parameters to 3x and 10x magnifications (was 2x, 4x, 8x)

Toughness

  • Upper glacis armor thickness increased and will now stop auto-cannon shell penetration

Upgrades / Modules

ABILITY MOD

  • Increase ATGM 385 damage → 650 damage
  • Increase canister penetration 82 mm → 97 mm
  • HESH explosion radius 6 m → 4.5m
  • HESH explosion damage 150 → 260
  • HESH damage multiplier against shell screens 2→ 0
  • HESH critical modules damage count 3 → 1
  • HESH damage modules count 1 → 3
  • HESH ammunition critical damage modifier 2 → 2.5
  • HESH engine critical damage modifier 1.45 → 1.7
  • HESH fuel critical damage modifier 1.25 → 1.4

  • ENLARGED GUN BREECH - Reduced Turret breech armor debuff 10% → 7.5%
  • ENLARGED GUN BREECH - Reduced amount of Conditional stacks to trigger Empowered 4 → 3
  • ENLARGED GUN BREECH - Conditional modifier Empowered now applies damage boost to explosion damage.
  • MEMENTO MORI - Removed incoming crit. multiplier
  • MEMENTO MORI - Added Repair Kit cooldown +20%
  • MEMENTO MORI -Reduced Repair Kit heal 50% → 20%

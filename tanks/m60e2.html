Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths and weaknesses based on community feedback

Strengths

Exceptional Gun Handling

The M60E2 offers exceptional gun handling characteristics. Its favorable ballistics and limited dispersion contribute to its effectiveness across various combat scenarios. Notable features include high accuracy while moving, good reload speed, and low damage falloff.

Strong Mobility

Despite not being the fastest, the M60E2 offers solid mobility, characterized by rapid acceleration, effortless top speed maintenance, and a good hull traverse. It can also achieve a temporary surge of extreme speed with its ‘Charge’ ability.

Favourable Hull Geometry

The M60E2's uniquely favorable hull geometry allows it to conquer obstacles that would stop other tanks. This grants its commanders the freedom to exploit unconventional pathways and areas to strike from unforeseen angles or positions.

Overpowering Torque

Thanks to the M60E2’s charge ability this vehicle can easily overmatch any opponent in a pushing match, Allowing M60’s to score environmental kills with ease. Alternatively your ramming damage is greatly augmented by this skill allowing you to deal heavy ramming damage without too much return damage to your own vehicle

Good Ability Synergy

Both of the M60E2’s regular abilities feature short cooldown timers and complement each other in close quarters. Players should use ‘Suppressive fire’ to fix an enemy in place and follow it up with ‘Charge’ to have a fast, decisive opening to most engagements.

Weaknesses

Limited HP Value

The M60E2 features a relatively low hitpoint pool at only 2500~ hit points meaning that any commander keeps a close eye on their health. Failure to manage a situation effectively will lead to a quick death as your staying power under fire is minimal.

Limited Reserve Energy

The M60E2’s abilities are relatively expensive at 45 and 25 energy points respectively. With a maximum energy pool of 100 and an energy regen speed of 0.8/s the M60 can very quickly find itself without its abilities. Players must be extra careful not to over rely on them and manage their usage effectively.

Glass Cannon

M60E2 features poor all round protection leaving it open to damaging shots from almost any opponent. Even autocannons may find ways to penetrate the tank. As a result players must be careful not to become the main focus of the enemy and adopt a more scavenger playstyle

Large Critical Zones

A large weakness is the M60E2’s vulnerability to critical hits. Almost the entire 1/3rd of the rear is taken up by ammo, engine and fuel critical zones protected by only token armour. Even from the front M60E2 presents a vulnerable ammo rack and fuel tank.

Lack of Shields

The M60E2 stands apart from many CW tanks by lacking any sacrificial shielding. As a result, commanders must be prepared to receive the full impact of enemy shots in almost all situations

Tank Summary

The M60E2 is a potentially powerful tank within Project CW. Players may find its lack of survivability in engagements compared to its alternatives discouraging. However, players who stick with the vehicle and learn its niches may find enjoyment in its playstyle.

The M60E2 is a close-quarters brawler and a glass cannon at the same time. As a result players should treat the M60E2 as a close second line scavenger and wait until your team fully engages the enemy before swooping in for fast, violent action and securing kills.

Overall the M60E2 is a High-Risk, High-Reward tank. Its playstyle is unlikely to appeal to many but its uncommonality on the battlefield may afford its player base more freedom as their enemy is likely to be unfamiliar with its strengths and weaknesses.

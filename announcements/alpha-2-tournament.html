Announcing Alpha 2 Tournament

Get ready everyone, showcase your skills, compete with the best, have a blast, and show who is the best at this game!

Tournament Announcement

Tournament Details:

  • Game mode: Plant/Defuse
  • Format: Teams of 5 players with 1 substitute (optional)
  • Teams: Up to 64

Dates:

  • 22 February 2025 - from February 22, 2025 9:00 PM
  • Winners Bracket - till round 5 inclusive
  • Losers Bracket - till round 5 inclusive
  • 23 February 2025 - from February 23, 2025 9:00 PM
  • Until the winner is determined

Sign-up

To sign up for the tournament, you will have to form a team, designate a captain, and sign up here:

  • Sign Up Form: https://surveys.wargaming.net/s3/Project-CW-Alpha-2-Tournament-Registration
  • Registration closes: February 20, 2025 2:00 AM or when all slots are filled. Slots are given on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Official tournament rules: https://docs.google.com/document/d/e/2PACX-1vRi1VEKT1KihVk70BUPHw-OhmwV6lJQqdyXu8nZX3FxHat-PK9wm2bpAchGOxHgiCd_CNJvNZQGr5uZ/pub

Prize Poll

The prize pool for each player in the winning teams is:

🥇 First Place

  • Project CW T-Shirt
  • Champion Role
  • $100 Amazon Voucher

🥈 Second Place

  • Project CW T-Shirt
  • $50 Amazon voucher

🥉 Third Place

  • Project CW T-Shirt

Let's make it unforgettable!

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users of our site. PCWStats does not use cookies, trackers, or any other methods to collect information about your browser type and version, operating system, referring site, or the date and time of access.

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information to use or analyze. Your privacy is respected and maintained by the design of our site.

PCWStats does not use any cookies or tracking technologies. We do not track your activities or collect any information about your usage of the site.

We do not collect personal data; hence, data security measures are not applicable for personal information. Our focus is on maintaining a secure website experience.

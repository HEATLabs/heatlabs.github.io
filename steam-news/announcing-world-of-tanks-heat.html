Announcing World of Tanks: HEAT

World of Tanks: HEAT

Introduction

Get ready to experience a bold new chapter in tactical vehicle combat. HEAT is officially coming to Steam, and whether you're a longtime fan of tank warfare or brand-new to the battlefield, this game is built to challenge your reflexes, test your strategy, and deliver intense team-based action like never before.

From the team behind one of the most iconic names in online vehicle combat, World of Tanks: HEAT takes the genre in a fresh new direction. This is not just about driving tanks. It’s about playing to your role, reading the battlefield, and making lightning-fast decisions under fire.

Watch the Official Reveal Trailer

Our brand-new trailer is out now, and it’s your first look at the vehicles, heroes, and chaos waiting for you on the front-lines. From high-speed flanks to dramatic last-second saves, every match is a tactical puzzle, and your crew is your greatest weapon.

Every vehicle in HEAT is piloted by an elite agent, each with their own backstory, playstyle, and unique active ability. Whether you're the type to hold the line, call the shots, or dive deep behind enemy lines, there's a role for you here.

World of Tanks: HEAT - Official Announcement Trailer

by World of Tanks: HEAT

Built for Competitive Play

World of Tanks: HEAT is designed from the ground up to feel tight, responsive, and intuitive. Expect intense matches that get to the action fast and reward strategy, squad-based tactics, and role mastery.

What to expect:

  • Fight across a wide range of dynamic maps
  • Coordinate with your squad to capture objectives and dominate control points
  • Master your favorite vehicles and unlock new looks and loadouts

No endless grinds. No pay-to-win. Just raw, skill-based competition, and all the chaos that comes with it.

Wishlist Now to Follow the Journey

Adding World of Tanks: HEAT to your Steam wishlist not only helps you stay on top of updates, it's also a great way to support our launch by boosting the game's visibility. Click the "Add to your wishlist" button on our Steam page to make sure you stay in the loop!

We're still in development, but big things are coming, and we want to make sure you're the first to know.

NVIDIA DLSS 4 with Multi Frame Generation and Reflex is coming to World of Tanks: HEAT. Providing the best performance and lowest latency possible.

Join the Community

Our official Discord is already live, and it's the best place to meet fellow squad members, ask questions, or just hang out with the devs. We’re active, we’re listening, and we’re building this experience with the help of the community every step of the way.

So if you're into tactical combat games, team-based strategy, or just driving a heavily-armored beast through a collapsing city block, this is your call to action.

Buckle up.

The HEAT is just getting started.

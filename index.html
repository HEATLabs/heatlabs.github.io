About PCWStats

PCWStats is a community-driven project aimed at providing Project CW players with comprehensive and reliable tank statistics and gameplay information. Our mission is to help players make informed decisions about which tanks to research and how to play them effectively.

Created by SINEWAVE and passionate players of Project CW, this platform aggregates data and presents it in an easy-to-understand format, allowing both new and experienced players to enhance their gameplay experience.

We're constantly working to improve and expand our database with the most accurate and up-to-date information. As this is a beta version, we welcome feedback and suggestions from the community.
